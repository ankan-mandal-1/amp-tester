Who is the world's richest woman in 2021?

Anshika Sharma
2:25:13 PM | Fri Jan 22 2021

With the rapid economic development, many giant companies have been born around the world. In addition to being very popular in their home countries, many companies have also achieved very good results in overseas markets, such as Huawei, Apple, Google, Amazon, Qualcomm, and other companies are like this, and these companies are also on the list of the world’s top 500 companies (Fortune 500). As the market value of these companies continues to rise so that their founders and senior managers are also earning money. Many people even became the richest man in the world because of this. Prior to this, the world’s richest man has always been Amazon’s CEO Bezos, and Amazon, as a comprehensive e-commerce giant, has developed very well in the US market, and also has a great reputation in overseas markets such as India, Canada, Japan, and South Korea. However, with the rapid rise in the field of new energy vehicles, the value of Elon Musk, the founder of Tesla's electric vehicles, has also skyrocketed, and Bezos, who has surpassed Amazon, has become the world's new richest man in 2021.

Whether it is Jeff Bezos or Elon Musk, they are almost all men; then who is the richest woman in the world? According to the 2021 Forbes Real-time Rich List, the world’s richest woman is France’s François Bettencourt-Meyers. She is the head of the French L'Oréal Group. Now, although François Béré Dangu Meyers is 67 years old, her worth is as high as 74.4 billion US dollars, which also makes Françoise Bettencourt Meyers the world’s most Rich women. Nowadays, when talking about the French L'Oreal Group, we are all familiar with it. This is almost a group company that specializes in women's business. In addition to the L'Oreal Paris brand, L'Oreal also has YSL, Lancome, Coyan's, Giorgio Armani, Maybelline, etc. A world-renowned brand and L'Oréal's business scope is spread all over the world. Globally, L'Oréal has more than 60,000 employees, which makes L'Oréal also one of the world's leading cosmetics groups.

As a leading company in the field of cosmetics and skincare products, the French L'Oréal Group has a history of more than one hundred years of development. This can be regarded as a century-old brand with a long history. At first, L'Oréal only developed and produced hair dyes. With the continuous expansion of the company’s business scope, L’Oréal eventually developed and expanded step by step, and acquired Lancome, Maybelline, and Helena globally, and officially began to enter the high-end cosmetics field.
